WHAT IS DREAM IN MEXICO?

Dream In Mexico is a legal non-profit organization that has helps young Mexican migrants, those still living in the US as well as those that have returned to Mexico, with access to higher education opportunities and professional development programs in Mexico.

OUR MISSION

Encourage and assist the repatriation of migrant Mexican youth by granting them with access to higher education opportunities and / or professional development programs in Mexico.

VISION

Be an institution of excellence that empowers Mexican migrant youth through the use of software technologies.
